# News

## 2025-08-22｜Foundation Officially Established
The Lin Huang-Yueh Ying Medical Foundation, Taipei, was officially registered in August 2025 and has since begun its mission to promote medical innovation and patient support. We sincerely appreciate the encouragement and support from all sectors and will continue striving to bring hope to patients.

---

## 2025-09-05-2025-09-07｜Invited to Speak at 2025 APASL STC Oncology
Dr. Chun-Yen Lin and Dr. Po-Ting Lin were invited to present at the Asia-Pacific Association for the Study of the Liver Single Topic Conference (APASL STC Oncology), sharing advances in early detection tools for liver cancer and fostering international academic and clinical exchange.

---

## 2025-10-25-2025-10-27｜Invited to Speak at the 2025 Asian Conference on Tumor Ablation (ACTA)
Dr. Po-Ting Lin was invited to deliver a lecture at the 2025 Asian Conference on Tumor Ablation (ACTA), presenting the latest advances and clinical experience in immunotherapy for hepatocellular carcinoma, and engaging in in-depth discussions with international experts, highlighting Taiwan’s contributions to liver cancer treatment.

---

## 2025-11-19-2025-11-21｜Invited to Chair Sessions at the International Liver Cancer Association (ILCA) Annual Conference
Dr. Po-Ting Lin was invited to chair two scientific sessions at the International Liver Cancer Association (ILCA) Annual Conference, contributing to international academic and clinical exchange in liver cancer and enhancing the global visibility of Taiwan’s medical community.

---

## 2026-01-17-2026-01-18｜Hosted the Liver Cancer and Hepatitis Academic Symposium with Chang Gung University and Chang Gung Liver Cancer Team
This symposium brought together experts from Chang Gung University and the Chang Gung Liver Cancer Team to discuss the latest developments in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of liver cancer and hepatitis.

## 2026-02-10｜Appreciation for the First Donations
The Foundation has received its first donations, and we sincerely thank all supporters for their generosity. These contributions will be used to advance clinical research and patient care, helping more families in need. We are committed to transparency and will regularly disclose how donations are utilized.
