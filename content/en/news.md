# Recent News

## 2026-03-15 | Foundation Officially Established

The Lin Huang Yueh-Ying Medical Foundation, Taipei, was officially registered in March 2026, formally commencing its mission to advance medical innovation and patient support. We are grateful for the encouragement and support from all our well-wishers and remain committed to bringing hope to patients and their families.

---

## 2026-03-01 | Official Website Launch

The foundation's official website is now live! The site provides information about our mission, founding story, founders, and donation methods. We welcome visitors to learn more about our work and vision. For any questions or suggestions, please contact us via email.

---

## 2026-02-20 | Dr. Chun-Yen Lin Delivers Keynote on Advances in Liver Cancer Immunotherapy

Dr. Chun-Yen Lin, Founder and Honorary Chairman, was invited to deliver a keynote address at the annual meeting of the Gastroenterological Society of Taiwan. He shared the latest clinical research findings and future outlook on immunotherapy for liver cancer. Professor Lin's research continues to open new treatment avenues for patients with liver disease.
