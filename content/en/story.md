# Our Story

Ms. Huang Yueh-Ying Lin was the beloved mother of Dr. Chun-Yen Lin and cherished grandmother of Dr. Po-Ting Lin. She was the most meaningful patient in their lives. For many years, Ms. Lin bravely endured liver cirrhosis and ultimately succumbed to liver cancer. To honor her love and spirit — and inspired by the profound sense of mission shaped through years of frontline clinical care and research — Dr. Lin established this foundation. Its purpose is to unite medical resources, foster clinical innovation, and elevate patient care quality, so that more families may find hope and face fewer regrets.
