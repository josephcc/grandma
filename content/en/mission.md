# Our Mission

The foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to advancing medical innovation and patient support. Its objectives are to:

- Strengthen resources for the diagnosis and treatment of major diseases and promote innovative therapies
- Support patient participation in clinical research and emerging treatments
- Foster interdisciplinary and cross-field development in clinical and basic medical sciences
- Establish an integrated healthcare platform that combines humanistic care with scientific advancement
